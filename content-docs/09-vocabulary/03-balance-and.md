# balance ... and ... 的用法

## 一句话先记住

`balance A and B` 表示“平衡 A 和 B”。

## 最常见结构

`balance + A + and + B`

例子：

- `balance work and life`
- `balance speed and safety`

## 例句

1. `Students should balance study and rest.`
2. `We need to balance cost and quality.`
3. `It is hard to balance work and family.`
4. `Good schools balance freedom and discipline.`

## 一个容易错的地方

不要少掉 `and`。

不要写：

`balance study rest`

要写：

`balance study and rest`

## 快速练习 1

选空：

1. We should balance work `____` health.  
   A. and  
   B. with

2. It is hard to balance speed `____` safety.  
   A. and  
   B. of

3. Parents often try to balance love `____` rules.  
   A. and  
   B. than

## 快速练习 2

把中文写成英文：

1. 我们需要平衡学习和休息。
2. 她在努力平衡工作和生活。
3. 这个计划平衡了成本和质量。

## 答案

### 快速练习 1

1. A
2. A
3. A

### 快速练习 2

1. `We need to balance study and rest.`
2. `She is trying to balance work and life.`
3. `The plan balances cost and quality.`

## 小结

这个短语最关键的不是意思，而是结构固定：

`balance A and B`
