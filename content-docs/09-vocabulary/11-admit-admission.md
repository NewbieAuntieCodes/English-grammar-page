# 📖 'admit' vs 'admission'

> Synced from web component: `src/components/content/Vocabulary/AdmitAdmissionUsageContent.tsx`

## Web Lesson Text

### 💡 为什么学这个？

### 📝 按意思分类学习

"admit" 是一个动词，而 "admission" 是它的名词形式。它们都与“承认”或“准许进入”有关，但词性决定了它们在句子中的位置和用法完全不同。

## Examples

1. `He **admitted** making a mistake.`
   - 他承认犯了个错误。(承认做某事: admit + V-ing)
2. `His silence was an **admission** of guilt.`
   - 他的沉默就是承认了罪行。
3. `She was **admitted** to the university.`
   - 她被这所大学录取了。
4. `**Admission** to the museum is free.`
   - 进入该博物馆是免费的。

## Sentence Builder Practice

1. Words: `He refused to` / `admit` / `his mistake`
   - Correct: `He refused to admit his mistake`
   - Chinese: 他拒绝承认自己的错误。
2. Words: `Admission to` / `the museum` / `is free`
   - Correct: `Admission to the museum is free`
   - Chinese: 进入博物馆是免费的。
3. Words: `His silence was` / `an admission of` / `guilt`
   - Correct: `His silence was an admission of guilt`
   - Chinese: 他的沉默就是承认有罪。
4. Words: `She was` / `admitted to` / `the university`
   - Correct: `She was admitted to the university`
   - Chinese: 她被这所大学录取了。

## Selector / Fill Practice

1. `She finally ____ that she had lied.`
   - Choices: **admitted** / admission
   - Chinese: 她最终承认她撒谎了。
2. `How much is the ____ fee for the park?`
   - Choices: **admission** / admit
   - Chinese: 这个公园的入场费是多少？
3. `He made a full ____ of his crimes to the police.`
   - Choices: **admission** / admit
   - Chinese: 他向警方全部承认了他的罪行。
4. `Only ticket holders will be ____ into the theater.`
   - Choices: **admitted** / admission
   - Chinese: 只有持票人才能被允许进入剧院。
