# admit vs. admission

## 一句话先记住

`admit` 常作动词，`admission` 常作名词。

## 最短区别

### admit

表示：

- 承认
- 允许进入

例子：

- `He admitted his mistake.`
- `Children are not admitted.`

### admission

表示：

- 承认
- 准入
- 入场费

例子：

- `admission to the school`
- `free admission`

## 例句

1. `She admitted that she was wrong.`
2. `He was admitted to hospital.`
3. `Admission to the museum is free today.`
4. `The college has strict admission rules.`

## 一个容易错的地方

不要把：

`He got admit to the club.`

写错词形。

更自然的是：

`He was admitted to the club.`

## 快速练习 1

选空：

1. He `____` breaking the window.  
   A. admitted  
   B. admission

2. `____` to the show is free.  
   A. Admit  
   B. Admission

3. She was `____` to the university.  
   A. admitted  
   B. admission

## 快速练习 2

把中文写成英文：

1. 他承认自己错了。
2. 进入这个博物馆不收费。
3. 她被这所大学录取了。

## 答案

### 快速练习 1

1. A
2. B
3. A

### 快速练习 2

1. `He admitted that he was wrong.`
2. `Admission to this museum is free.`
3. `She was admitted to this university.`

## 小结

做题时先看句子里缺的是动作还是名词。
