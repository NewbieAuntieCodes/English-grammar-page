# 宾语从句 / Object Clauses

## 一句话先记住

`宾语从句就是把一个小句子放在动词后面，当宾语用。`

## 它为什么叫“宾语从句”？

先看：

`I know the answer.`

这里 `the answer` 是宾语。

再看：

`I know that he is right.`

这里 `that he is right` 放在 `know` 后面，也是在充当宾语。

所以它叫宾语从句。

## 常见位置

`主语 + 动词 + 宾语从句`

比如：

- `I think that she is right.`
- `He says that he is busy.`
- `Do you know where she lives?`

## 常见引导词

### 1. `that`

表示“一个事实”。

例子：

- `I think that he is honest.`
- `She says that she is tired.`

### 2. `if / whether`

表示“是否”。

例子：

- `I don't know if he will come.`
- `She asked whether I was free.`

### 3. `what / who / where / when / why / how`

表示具体信息。

例子：

- `Tell me what you want.`
- `I know where he works.`
- `She asked why you left.`

## 最重要的一条规则

`宾语从句里面通常用陈述句语序。`

对比：

- `Where is he?` 问句
- `I know where he is.` 宾语从句

不要写成：

`I know where is he.`

## 例句

1. `I believe that she can do it.`
2. `We don't know if they are at home.`
3. `Can you tell me where the station is?`
4. `Nobody knows why he is angry.`

## 一个容易错的地方

不要把宾语从句里面再写成问句语序。

比如：

- `Do you know what he wants?` 对
- `Do you know what does he want?` 不对

## 快速练习 1

判断下面画线部分是不是宾语从句：

1. I think **that he is kind**.
2. The news **that he told me** is true.
3. She asked **where Tom was**.
4. **What he said** surprised me.

## 快速练习 2

选出正确句子：

1. A. I know where she is.  
   B. I know where is she.

2. A. He asked if I was busy.  
   B. He asked if was I busy.

3. A. Tell me what you need.  
   B. Tell me what do you need.

## 答案

### 快速练习 1

1. 是
2. 不是，这是定语从句
3. 是
4. 不是，这是主语从句

### 快速练习 2

1. A
2. A
3. A

## 小结

宾语从句最关键的不是名字，而是两个判断：

- 它是不是放在动词后面
- 它里面是不是用了陈述句语序
