# 定语从句 / Attributive Clauses

## 一句话先记住

`定语从句就是用一个小句子去修饰前面的名词。`

## 先看最短例子

`The boy who is running is Tom.`

这里：

- 被修饰的名词是 `the boy`
- `who is running` 在说明“哪个男孩”

所以 `who is running` 是定语从句。

## 学定语从句先看哪里？

第一步，不是先看 `who / which / that`。

第一步是先找：

`它前面那个名词是谁。`

这个名词常常叫先行词。

## 常见引导词

### 修饰人

- `who`
- `whom`
- `that`
- `whose`

例子：

- `The girl who sings well is my cousin.`
- `The man whom you met is my uncle.`
- `The boy whose bike is new is Tom.`

### 修饰物

- `which`
- `that`

例子：

- `The book that is on the desk is mine.`
- `I like the movie which we watched yesterday.`

### 修饰地点或时间

- `where`
- `when`

例子：

- `This is the park where we play.`
- `I remember the day when we met.`

## 怎么认定语从句？

问自己两个问题：

1. 这部分是不是在说明前面的名词？
2. 去掉这部分后，主句还在不在？

比如：

`The book that is on the desk is mine.`

去掉 `that is on the desk` 后，主句还是：

`The book is mine.`

所以中间那部分是在修饰 `book`。

## 例句

1. `The teacher who helped me is kind.`
2. `The phone that I bought is cheap.`
3. `This is the house where he lives.`
4. `I will never forget the day when we met.`

## 一个容易错的地方

不要把定语从句和宾语从句混在一起。

对比：

`I know that he is right.`

- `that he is right` 在 `know` 后面
- 是宾语从句

`The idea that he gave me was useful.`

- `that he gave me` 在修饰 `idea`
- 是定语从句

## 快速练习 1

找出下面句子里的先行词：

1. The girl who is wearing red is my sister.
2. The book that you lent me is interesting.
3. This is the place where we had lunch.
4. I remember the year when he moved here.

## 快速练习 2

判断下面画线部分是不是定语从句：

1. The boy **who won the game** is happy.
2. I know **who won the game**.
3. The reason **why he left** is clear.
4. She said **that she was tired**.

## 答案

### 快速练习 1

1. `girl`
2. `book`
3. `place`
4. `year`

### 快速练习 2

1. 是
2. 不是，这是宾语从句
3. 是
4. 不是，这是宾语从句

## 小结

定语从句最关键的一步是先找先行词。

只要看出它是在说明前面的名词，就不容易判断错。
