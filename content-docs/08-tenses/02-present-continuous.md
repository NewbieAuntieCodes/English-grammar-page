# 现在进行时 / Present Continuous

## 一句话先记住

`现在进行时表示现在正在发生的动作。`

## 最常见信号词

- now
- right now
- at the moment
- look
- listen

## 最常见结构

`主语 + am / is / are + 动词-ing`

比如：

- `I am reading now.`
- `She is cooking.`
- `They are playing football.`

## be 动词怎么选？

- I → am
- he / she / it → is
- we / you / they → are

## 例句

1. `I am doing my homework now.`
2. `Look! The baby is sleeping.`
3. `They are waiting for the bus.`
4. `We are having lunch at the moment.`

## 一个容易错的地方

不要只写 `doing`，忘记前面的 `be`。

比如：

- `She reading a book now.` 不对
- `She is reading a book now.` 对

## 快速练习 1

选空：

1. He `____` TV now.  
   A. watches  
   B. is watching

2. We `____` lunch at the moment.  
   A. are having  
   B. had

3. I `____` to music now.  
   A. am listening  
   B. listen

## 快速练习 2

把中文写成英文：

1. 我现在正在写作业。
2. 他们正在打扫教室。
3. 看！天正在下雨。

## 答案

### 快速练习 1

1. B
2. A
3. A

### 快速练习 2

1. `I am doing my homework now.`
2. `They are cleaning the classroom.`
3. `Look! It is raining.`

## 小结

看到 `now`、`look` 这类提示词时，很常要想到：

`be + 动词-ing`
