# 一般过去时 / Simple Past Tense

## 一句话先记住

`一般过去时表示过去发生并且已经结束的动作或状态。`

## 最常见信号词

- yesterday
- last night
- last week
- ago
- in 2020

## 最常见结构

### 1. 动作动词

`主语 + 动词过去式`

- `I watched TV last night.`
- `She visited her grandma yesterday.`

### 2. be 动词

- `I was tired.`
- `They were happy.`

## 规则动词和不规则动词

### 规则动词

常常直接加 `-ed`：

- work → worked
- play → played

### 不规则动词

要单独记：

- go → went
- eat → ate
- see → saw

## 例句

1. `He cleaned his room yesterday.`
2. `We went to the park last Sunday.`
3. `I was busy this morning.`
4. `They had lunch at school.`

## 一个容易错的地方

不要看到过去时间词，却还用原形。

比如：

- `I go there yesterday.` 不对
- `I went there yesterday.` 对

## 快速练习 1

选空：

1. She `____` home late last night.  
   A. comes  
   B. came

2. They `____` very happy yesterday.  
   A. were  
   B. are

3. I `____` an apple this morning.  
   A. ate  
   B. eat

## 快速练习 2

把中文写成英文：

1. 我昨天打了篮球。
2. 他上周去了上海。
3. 她那时很累。

## 答案

### 快速练习 1

1. B
2. A
3. A

### 快速练习 2

1. `I played basketball yesterday.`
2. `He went to Shanghai last week.`
3. `She was very tired then.`

## 小结

看到明确的过去时间，先优先想到一般过去时。
