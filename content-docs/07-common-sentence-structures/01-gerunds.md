# 动词-ing 的用法 / Gerunds

## 一句话先记住

`动词-ing` 常常可以像名词一样用。

## 最常见的 3 种用法

### 1. 作主语

- `Reading is fun.`
- `Swimming is good exercise.`

### 2. 作宾语

- `I enjoy reading.`
- `She likes singing.`

### 3. 放在介词后面

- `He is good at drawing.`
- `Thank you for helping me.`

## 最短提醒

- 很多动词后面接 `doing`
- 介词后面常常也接 `doing`
- 先把高频句子记住，不用一次背完整张表

## 一个容易错的地方

不要写：

`He is good at draw.`

要写：

`He is good at drawing.`

因为 `at` 是介词，后面常常接 `doing`。

## 例句

1. `Learning English takes time.`
2. `I enjoy playing basketball.`
3. `She left without saying goodbye.`
4. `My hobby is collecting stamps.`

## 快速练习 1

选空：

1. I enjoy `____` books.  
   A. read  
   B. reading

2. She is interested in `____` music.  
   A. listening to  
   B. listen to

3. `____` early is a good habit.  
   A. Getting up  
   B. Get up

## 快速练习 2

把中文写成英文：

1. 游泳很有趣。
2. 我喜欢听音乐。
3. 他离开时没有说再见。

## 答案

### 快速练习 1

1. B
2. A
3. A

### 快速练习 2

1. `Swimming is fun.`
2. `I like listening to music.`
3. `He left without saying goodbye.`

## 小结

学 `doing` 时，先抓最常见环境：

- 句首作主语
- 动词后
- 介词后
