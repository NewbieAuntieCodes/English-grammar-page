# as...as 的用法 / as...as

## 一句话先记住

`as + 形容词 / 副词原级 + as` 表示“和……一样……”。

## 最常见结构

- `as tall as`
- `as fast as`
- `as carefully as`

例子：

- `Tom is as tall as Jack.`
- `She runs as fast as her brother.`

## 否定形式

- `not as ... as`

例子：

- `This book is not as easy as that one.`

## 例句

1. `He is as busy as his father.`
2. `Lucy sings as well as Mary.`
3. `My bag is not as heavy as yours.`
4. `This room is as clean as that one.`

## 一个容易错的地方

中间通常放原级，不放比较级。

所以：

- `as taller as` 不对
- `as tall as` 才对

## 快速练习 1

选空：

1. He is as `____` as Tom.  
   A. taller  
   B. tall

2. She writes as `____` as her teacher.  
   A. carefully  
   B. more carefully

3. This box is not as `____` as that one.  
   A. heavy  
   B. heavier

## 快速练习 2

把中文写成英文：

1. 她和我一样高。
2. 他跑得和你一样快。
3. 这道题没有那道题难。

## 答案

### 快速练习 1

1. B
2. A
3. A

### 快速练习 2

1. `She is as tall as I am.`
2. `He runs as fast as you do.`
3. `This question is not as difficult as that one.`

## 小结

`as...as` 里最关键的一点就是：

中间放原级。
