# keep 的用法 / keep

## 一句话先记住

`keep` 常见意思是“保持”或“继续”。

## 最常见的两种结构

### 1. `keep + 形容词`

- `Keep quiet.`
- `Please keep the door open.`

### 2. `keep + doing`

- `He kept talking.`
- `She kept smiling.`

## 例句

1. `Please keep calm.`
2. `Keep the window closed.`
3. `He kept asking the same question.`
4. `They kept working after dinner.`

## 一个容易错的地方

不要写：

`He kept to talk.`

这里更自然的是：

`He kept talking.`

## 快速练习 1

选空：

1. Please keep `____`.  
   A. quiet  
   B. quietly

2. She kept `____` all night.  
   A. study  
   B. studying

3. Keep the door `____`.  
   A. open  
   B. opening

## 快速练习 2

把中文写成英文：

1. 请保持安静。
2. 他一直在笑。
3. 请让灯一直开着。

## 答案

### 快速练习 1

1. A
2. B
3. A

### 快速练习 2

1. `Please keep quiet.`
2. `He kept laughing.`
3. `Please keep the light on.`

## 小结

看到 `keep`，常先想两个模板：

- `keep + adj.`
- `keep + doing`
