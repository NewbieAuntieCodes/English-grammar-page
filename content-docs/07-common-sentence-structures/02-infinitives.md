# 不定式的用法 / Infinitives

## 一句话先记住

不定式最常见的样子是 `to + 动词原形`。

## 最常见的 4 种用法

### 1. 动词后面

- `I want to go home.`
- `She hopes to see you.`

### 2. 表示目的

- `He got up early to catch the bus.`
- `She went out to buy milk.`

### 3. 作表语

- `My dream is to be a doctor.`

### 4. 修饰名词

- `I have homework to do.`
- `She needs a place to live.`

## 最短提醒

- `to do` 不等于一般将来时
- 它常常只是一个句子部件
- 很多动词后面直接接 `to do`

## 一个容易错的地方

不要写：

`I want go home.`

要写：

`I want to go home.`

## 例句

1. `They decided to stay.`
2. `I came here to help you.`
3. `His plan is to study abroad.`
4. `Do you have anything to drink?`

## 快速练习 1

选空：

1. She wants `____` English.  
   A. learn  
   B. to learn

2. I went to the shop `____` some bread.  
   A. buy  
   B. to buy

3. I have a lot of work `____`.  
   A. to do  
   B. doing

## 快速练习 2

把中文写成英文：

1. 我希望再次见到你。
2. 他努力学习是为了通过考试。
3. 我有一些问题要问。

## 答案

### 快速练习 1

1. B
2. B
3. A

### 快速练习 2

1. `I hope to see you again.`
2. `He studies hard to pass the exam.`
3. `I have some questions to ask.`

## 小结

`to do` 最先记住两个感觉就够用很多：

- 接在很多动词后面
- 常常表示“为了……”
