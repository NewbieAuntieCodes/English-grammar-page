# 介词 by 的用法 / by

## 一句话先记住

`by` 常见意思有“通过”“在……旁边”“到……之前”。

## 最常见的几种用法

### 1. 交通方式

- `by bus`
- `by train`

### 2. 通过某种方式

- `learn by reading`
- `send it by email`

### 3. 在……旁边

- `sit by the window`

### 4. 到……之前

- `finish it by Friday`

## 例句

1. `I go to school by bus.`
2. `She learned English by watching films.`
3. `He sat by the door.`
4. `Please finish the work by tomorrow.`

## 一个容易错的地方

不要把：

`by bus`

写成：

`with bus`

交通方式常用 `by`。

## 快速练习 1

选空：

1. We traveled `____` train.  
   A. by  
   B. with

2. He is standing `____` the window.  
   A. by  
   B. of

3. I must finish this `____` Sunday.  
   A. by  
   B. with

## 快速练习 2

把中文写成英文：

1. 我骑自行车上学。
2. 她通过练习来提高英语。
3. 请在今晚之前回我消息。

## 答案

### 快速练习 1

1. A
2. A
3. A

### 快速练习 2

1. `I go to school by bike.`
2. `She improves her English by practicing.`
3. `Please reply to me by tonight.`

## 小结

`by` 常见但不乱。

先抓最实用的 3 个：

- `by + 交通`
- `by + 方法`
- `by + 时间`
