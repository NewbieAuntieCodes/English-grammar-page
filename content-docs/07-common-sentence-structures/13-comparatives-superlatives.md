# 比较级和最高级 / Comparatives and Superlatives

## 一句话先记住

`比较级` 用来比两个，`最高级` 用来比三个或以上。

## 最常见形式

### 比较级

- tall → taller
- fast → faster
- beautiful → more beautiful

### 最高级

- tall → tallest
- fast → fastest
- beautiful → most beautiful

## 例句

1. `Tom is taller than Jack.`
2. `This car is faster than that one.`
3. `Lucy is the tallest girl in the class.`
4. `This is the most interesting book here.`

## 常见搭配

- `比较级 + than`
- `the + 最高级`

## 一个容易错的地方

最高级前面常常有 `the`。

所以：

- `He is tallest in our class.` 不自然
- `He is the tallest in our class.` 更对

## 快速练习 1

选空：

1. My brother is `____` than me.  
   A. tall  
   B. taller

2. This is the `____` street in town.  
   A. busiest  
   B. busier

3. English is `____` than math for me.  
   A. easier  
   B. easiest

## 快速练习 2

把中文写成英文：

1. 今天比昨天热。
2. 她是我们班最勤奋的学生。
3. 这本书比那本书更有趣。

## 答案

### 快速练习 1

1. B
2. A
3. A

### 快速练习 2

1. `Today is hotter than yesterday.`
2. `She is the most hard-working student in our class.`
3. `This book is more interesting than that one.`

## 小结

记忆时先抓两个信号：

- `than` 常提醒你比较级
- `the` 常提醒你最高级
