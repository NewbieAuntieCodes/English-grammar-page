# Good / Well

## 一句话先记住

`good` 常是形容词，`well` 常是副词。

## 两种常见用法

### 1. good

- She is a good student.
- This book is good.

这里 `good` 在描述人或东西，所以是形容词。

### 2. well

- She sings well.
- He speaks English well.

这里 `well` 在描述动作，所以是副词。

## 一个要额外记住的点

有时候：

- I am well.

这里 `well` 表示“身体好”，也很常见。

## 快速练习 1

选 `good` 还是 `well`：

1. She is a ___ teacher.
2. He plays basketball ___.
3. I feel ___ today.
4. This movie is ___.

## 快速练习 2

判断下面哪个更对：

1. She sings good / well.
2. He is a good / well boy.
3. I am good / well today.

## 答案

### 快速练习 1

1. good
2. well
3. well
4. good

### 快速练习 2

1. well
2. good
3. well
