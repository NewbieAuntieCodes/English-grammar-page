# Slow / Slowly

## 一句话先记住

`slow` 常是形容词，`slowly` 常是副词。

## 两种用法

### 1. slow

- The bus is slow.
- a slow computer

这里 `slow` 在描述人或东西，所以是形容词。

### 2. slowly

- Please speak slowly.
- He walked slowly.

这里 `slowly` 在描述动作，所以是副词。

## 快速练习 1

选 `slow` 还是 `slowly`：

1. The car is very ___.
2. Please read ___.
3. We had a ___ internet connection.
4. She answered ___.

## 快速练习 2

判断下面哪个更对：

1. He walks slow / slowly.
2. It is a slow / slowly train.

## 答案

### 快速练习 1

1. slow
2. slowly
3. slow
4. slowly

### 快速练习 2

1. slowly
2. slow
