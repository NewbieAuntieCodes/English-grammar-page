# 代词 / Pronouns

## 一句话先记住

`代词是代替名词的词。`

## 怎么认代词？

如果一个词是在代替前面已经出现的人或东西，它常常就是代词。

常见代词有：

- I
- you
- he
- she
- it
- we
- they

## 例子

1. Tom is my friend. He is funny.
2. This is my book. It is new.
3. Lucy and I are classmates. We study together.

这些代词有：

- He
- It
- We

## 最短提醒

- 代词能避免重复说同一个名词
- 主格和宾格先简单感受，不必一次讲完

## 快速练习 1

把重复名词换成代词：

1. Tom is tall. Tom is kind.
2. The dog is small. The dog is cute.
3. Lucy and Lily are friends. Lucy and Lily play together.

## 快速练习 2

下面哪个词是代词？

1. She likes music.
   - A. She
   - B. likes
   - C. music

2. The book is on the table. It is mine.
   - A. table
   - B. It
   - C. mine

3. My father works hard.
   - A. My
   - B. father
   - C. 没有代词

## 小结

代词是在“替别人出场”。

## 答案

### 快速练习 1

1. Tom is tall. He is kind.
2. The dog is small. It is cute.
3. Lucy and Lily are friends. They play together.

### 快速练习 2

1. A
2. B
